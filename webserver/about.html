About Us

The Birdfeeder Collective is a team consisting of three students from Lund University, Sweden. We are participating in 2022 IEEE AP-S Student Design Contest, where we will build a smart birdfeeder. This is our website, and here you can read about our project and analyze your birdfeeder.
